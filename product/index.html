OODA BOND 109 Clear & Ultra Clear Epoxy is a fast-curing, high-strength bonder. It is a versatile and easy-to-use two-part epoxy adhesive suitable for coating, potting, casting, bonding, or repairing most materials. Typical applications include electronic components, medical devices, electrical products, and more. OODA BOND 109 Potting and Encapsulating Compound is a two-part, economical, flowable, electronic-grade epoxy that provides excellent physical, chemical, and electrical protection.
